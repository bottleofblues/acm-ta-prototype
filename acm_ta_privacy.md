# Paul Bickford Solutions • Advanced Career Mastery — Data Use Summary

**Purpose.** We use learner-provided inputs (personal website URLs, LinkedIn text/URL, job description, Day-90 outcomes)
solely to personalize the first-90-days learning plan, exercises, and check-ins.

**Consent.** Each data category is opt-in. If you decline, you can proceed via manual entry.

**Storage.** Content is stored securely, access-limited to program staff and the learner's TA.

**LinkedIn.** We do not automate scraping. Learners paste content or share exports per LinkedIn's terms.

**Deletion.** Learners can request deletion at any time; confirmed within 7 business days.

**Security.** Encryption in transit; encryption at rest on compliant infrastructure.
